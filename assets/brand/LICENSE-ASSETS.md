## Overview

The files in this directory (`/assets/brand/`) contain DuckBrain's brand identity materials, including but not limited to:

- **Logo** (`logo/`) - Project logo and variants
- **Mascot** (`mascot/`) - Character artwork
- **Banner** (`banner/`) - Promotional images
- Any other brand-related visual assets

**These assets are NOT covered by the Apache 2.0 license** that applies to the rest of the DuckBrain codebase. They are provided under a separate license to protect the project's visual identity.

## Exceptions and Special Permissions

We understand there may be legitimate use cases not covered by CC BY-NC-ND. We're open to granting exceptions on a case-by-case basis for:

- **Educational content** (tutorials, courses, blog posts)
- **Conference presentations** and talks about DuckBrain
- **Community events** and hackathons
- **Non-commercial forks** that need logo variants

**To request an exception:**
1. Open an issue with the `license-exception` label
2. Describe your use case
3. Specify which assets you need and how you'll use them
4. We'll respond within 7 business days
